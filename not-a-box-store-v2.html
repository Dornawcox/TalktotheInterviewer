Build Your Own Box

Everything you need to assemble open infrastructure for your community — no experience required.

1

Gather Your Hardware

Source the components below. Total cost is typically $250-300. All parts are off-the-shelf and globally available.

See Bill of Materials below ↓
2

Flash the Boot Media

Download our pre-configured image and flash it to your NVMe SSD or SD card using the Raspberry Pi Imager.

wget https://box.farmhack.community/images/farmhack-box-v1.img.xz
xzcat farmhack-box-v1.img.xz | sudo dd of=/dev/nvme0n1 bs=4M status=progress
3

Assemble the Hardware

Connect the NVMe HAT, attach the RFID reader, and mount in the enclosure. No soldering required.

Pi 5 → NVMe HAT (M.2 slot) → SSD
Pi 5 GPIO → RC522 RFID Reader (SPI pins)
Enclosure → Mount standoffs → Close
4

First Boot Ceremony

Power on and complete the setup wizard. Name your box, connect to your network, and join the federation.

1. Connect Ethernet and power
2. Find your box at http://farmhack.local
3. Create admin account
4. Configure domain (optional)
5. Tap RFID card to activate (gift economy mode)
5

Join the Network

Your box automatically discovers peers and begins syncing the Commons Registry. You're now part of the Open Box Network!

✓ Federation JSON published
✓ Mirror sync configured
✓ Backup schedule active
✓ Welcome email sent
